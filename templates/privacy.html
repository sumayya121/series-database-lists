    6. Our Cookie Policy

    We only use strictly necessary session cookies that are essential for the website to function correctly.

    • What are cookies? Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer or smartphone) when you visit our website.
    • What we use: We use a single session cookie to manage your login state. This cookie is temporary and is automatically deleted when you close your web browser.
    • No Consent Required: Under EU and UK data protection law (GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive), cookies that are strictly necessary for the provision of a service explicitly requested by the user do not require prior consent. You can still manage or block cookies via your browser settings, but please note that the website will not function correctly if you block essential session cookies.
